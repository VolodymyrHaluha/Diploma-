# **App Name**: ZenithFit

## Core Features:

- Responsive Marketing Pages: Showcase key club information across a fully responsive design, including a striking hero section, club overview, membership details, and an introduction to classes.
- Dynamic Membership Plans Display: Present detailed membership tiers with features and clear calls-to-action to help potential members choose their plan.
- Personal Trainer Profiles: Display dedicated profile cards for personal trainers, including their photo, specialty, and experience.
- Interactive Class Schedule: Provide an intuitive and interactive weekly schedule table for members to view and filter upcoming classes.
- Club News & Promotions Feed: A dynamic section featuring the latest club announcements, promotions, fitness articles, and events, allowing administrative updates.
- AI-Assisted Content Tool: An administrative tool to assist club staff in generating engaging news post drafts or summarizing fitness articles for the club's news feed.
- Integrated Contact & Footer: A comprehensive contact section with a functional form, interactive map, address, working hours, and social media links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant electric blue (#66CCFF), chosen for its modern, energetic, and professional qualities, reminiscent of a 'neon' glow against dark elements.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated bluish-grey (#141B1F), providing a sleek and premium backdrop for content and highlights the primary accent colors.
- Accent color: An eye-catching emerald green-aqua (#26D9BA), providing strong contrast with the dark background and primary blue, aligning with themes of vitality and tech.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy, and bold statement. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean readability and a neutral, objective feel in longer text blocks.
- Sleek and minimalistic vector icons with subtle gradients or a 'neon' outline effect to complement the dark luxury aesthetic and interactive elements.
- Utilize large hero sections with full-width imagery or video backgrounds, coupled with modern 'glassmorphism' card designs and strategic 5px margins to create spacious and premium content blocks. Emphasis on a responsive grid for optimal viewing across all devices.
- Implement smooth transitions for navigation and content loading, subtle hover effects on interactive elements, and captivating smooth scrolling animations to enhance user experience and maintain a dynamic, premium feel.
